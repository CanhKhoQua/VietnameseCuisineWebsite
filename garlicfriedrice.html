Garlic Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients: (4 servings)

- 4-5 cups of cooked leftover Jasmine rice

- 3 medium eggs

- 1 bulb of garlic

- Salt, sugar, vinegar, fish sauce and spring onion for garnishing

Directions

- Peel garlic, finely mince 3 small cloves, and slightly smash the rest.

- In a wok, gently fry the smashed garlic cloves with oil under medium low heat until slightly golden. We will need around 2 tbs of garlic oil for this fried rice, you can save the garlic oil for marinating or stir frying. It tastes wonderful! The fried garlic cloves can be eaten as well, great immune system boost. If not, you can use it to cook other dishes, or simply add soya sauce, chili and a little bit of sugar to make a great dipping sauce!

- Crack eggs, add ½ tsp salt and 1 tsp vinegar. Beat and fry under medium heat, then use spatula to separate the eggs into very small pieces.

- Add rice to stir fry, minced garlic next and season with around ½ tsp salt or adjust accordingly. Add 1tbs of garlic oil (or more if desired) and lastly add ½ tsp of sugar, ground peppers to taste, 1.5 tsp of fish sauce. Mix and toss well to combine everything together. Serve hot with chopped spring onions.
