About me

I'm Loc Nguyen, the owner of this site. The only reason I created this website is that I want to help people reach out Vietnamese food recipes with the only wish that people around the world would know more about the variety of Vietnamese cuisine. Vietnamese cuisine is not wrapped up only in Pho or Banh Mi. Vietnamese food is more than that.
